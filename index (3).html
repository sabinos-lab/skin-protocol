Hyaluronic Acid Rule

Always on damp skin as the very first serum, every AM and PM. HA needs ambient moisture to work — on dry skin it draws moisture out. On tretinoin nights it significantly reduces irritation.

Vitamin C Rules

  • Morning only — works synergistically with SPF for photoprotection
  • Mix Resveratrol drops into Vit C in palm before applying
  • Store in fridge — oxidises at room temperature
  • Never at night before tretinoin
  • Never same session as GHK-Cu

Device Rules — 4-3 Split

  • LED nights (4) — Mon / Tue / Thu (no, exfoliation) — corrected: Mon / Tue / Fri / Sat · Always first after cleansing on bare skin
  • Control Pro nights (3) — Wed / Fri (needling only) / Sun · Always with conductive gel, always last step
  • Eye Wand — every AM + PM, 2 min each eye over eye cream
  • Dennis Gross LED — Mon / Tue / Sat, red 10–15 min, bare skin after cleanse
  • Project E LED face + neck — Fri (non-needling), red + NIR 15 min, bare skin
  • Derma Roller 1mm — every 2–3 Fridays, clean with 70% alcohol, no LED same night
