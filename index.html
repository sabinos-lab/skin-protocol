Protocol
Core Rules & Devices
The non-negotiables that make this routine work safely and effectively. These are based on ingredient chemistry and device science — not preferences.

Tretinoin Rules

  • Always apply HA first on damp skin, wait 2 min
  • Wait 20–30 min after applying before next step
  • Never same night as any acid
  • Never same night as Vitamin C serums
  • Mon / Wed / Sun = minimum 48hr between each

Ingredient Conflicts

  • GHK-Cu + Vitamin C = never together — oxidise each other
  • Tretinoin + acids = never same night
  • AHA Peel + Glycolic = never same night
  • No devices on Thursday post-exfoliation
  • No LED, acids or Control Pro on microneedling Fridays

Hyaluronic Acid Rule

Always on damp skin as the very first serum, every AM and PM without exception. HA needs ambient moisture to work — on dry skin it draws moisture out instead of in. On tretinoin nights it also significantly reduces irritation and barrier disruption.

Vitamin C Rules

  • Morning only — works synergistically with SPF
  • Mix Resveratrol drops directly into Vit C in palm
  • Store in fridge — oxidises at room temperature
  • Never used at night before tretinoin
  • Never on same session as GHK-Cu
Devices
Verified Device Schedule

Dr Levy Eye Wand

Every morning and every evening, after eye product is applied. 2 minutes per eye. Outward/upward motions along orbital bone and into marionette folds. Always use before thick creams. Working medium: Dr Levy Eye Booster Concentrate.

Dr Levy The Control Pro

Monday + Wednesday nights only. Always apply Dr Levy Conductive Gel across full face first — without it current cannot travel effectively and friction may damage skin. Full face EMS/microcurrent 5–10 min. Wipe off gel after. Reapply moisturiser to reseal. Never on Thursday or microneedling Fridays.

Dr Dennis Gross LED Mask

Tuesday + Saturday nights. RED light setting, 10–15 min. Use BLUE light only if active congestion is present. Always used at night — morning LED is counteracted by subsequent UV exposure. No medium required.

Project E LED — Face + Neck

Non-microneedling Fridays only. RED + Near-Infrared setting, 15 min. This is the priority LED night of the week — neck coverage addresses a critical and often neglected area of mature skin loss. No medium required.

Derma Roller 1mm

Every 2–3 Fridays only. Clean with 70% alcohol before and after every single use. 4 directions, light pressure, full face. Apply VT Reedle Shot + GHK-Cu immediately after. No LED, no acids, no tretinoin, no Control Pro on needling nights.

Storage Notes

Fridge: Medicube Vit C products, Numbuzin NAD+ Essence, Dr Levy Freezing Anti-Fatigue Mask.

Away from light and heat: Tretinoin, GHK-Cu Serum.

Store separately: Keep GHK-Cu away from all Vitamin C products at all times.
